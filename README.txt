## Supported commands:

topic			: tells the topics that the bot supported

tellme <topic>		: gives last feed of <topic>

subscribe <topic>	: subscribe to <topic>

unsubscribe <topic>	: unsubscribe <topic>

language <lang>		: change feeds' language to <lang>
			  supported language:
			    'eng': English
			    'cht': Traditional Chinese
			    'chs': Simplified Chinese
